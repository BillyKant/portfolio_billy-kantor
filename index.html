About Me

Hi, I live in Rushville, Indiana with my wife Sarah, son Joey, and our Cat Remington. I grew up outside of Washington D.C., and after I went to college for civil engineering started working in construction. Throughout my time in construction I have moved all over the midwest, living in places like Kansas City, and Columbus. I made the switch to coding because I love to solve problems and get creative! I would ideally love to meet my love of farming or experience in construction to my new career in programming.
